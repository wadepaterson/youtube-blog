Introducing Channel Memberships: Three Tiers of Support and What You Get

By Wade Paterson

Exciting news — there's a brand new membership option available on the channel! You can find it by clicking the Join button underneath any video. But before diving into the details, let me explain exactly what this is, who it's for, and what each tier offers. Most importantly, let me start with something I feel strongly about.

A Note Before Anything Else: This Is Entirely Optional

If you do not have the funds to afford a membership, please do not even think about signing up. That's not what this is for. I will continue to put out great free content on this channel, just as I always have. That commitment isn't changing.

By simply watching videos, subscribing, commenting, and liking, you already support this channel in a meaningful way — and I appreciate every bit of it. If membership isn't something you're interested in, that's perfectly fine. Go enjoy the library of free content and everything that's still to come. Full stop.

However, for those who are looking for a little more premium access, don't mind paying for some additional perks, and have the means to do so, membership is a wonderful way to go deeper. Your support helps me focus more time and energy on creating valuable content. So let me break down each of the three tiers.

Bronze Membership: Stand Out and Get Heard

The Bronze tier is by far the most affordable option — less than the price of a cup of coffee per month. Here's what you get:

  • Loyalty Badges: Every time you comment on one of my videos, you'll stand out from the crowd with a special badge that signifies your support.
  • Guaranteed Priority Response to Comments: I work hard to respond to as many comments as possible, but as the channel grows, that becomes increasingly challenging. Bronze members have my dedication — I will respond to your comments.

Silver Membership: Early Access, Exclusive Content, and More

The Silver tier includes everything in the Bronze membership, plus a significant upgrade in access and content. Here's what's added:

  • Early Access to Videos: You'll see new videos before anyone else as a thank-you for your support.
  • Member-Only Videos: Think special surprises — such as interviews with powerful, impactful public speakers — that won't be available on the main channel. These are the kinds of projects I want to create and deliver specifically to the people who are helping drive this channel forward.
  • Periodic Shout-Outs: I plan to give regular recognition to Silver-level supporters.
  • Exclusive Social Feed: Members-only messages, photos, updates, tips, and advice — all accessible through the membership feed.

Gold Membership: A Personal Connection

The Gold tier is the ultimate level of support, and I want to stress — only sign up for this if you can absolutely afford it. I do not want anyone pushing themselves financially. But if it works for you, here's what you receive:

  • All Bronze and Silver Perks: Loyalty badges, priority comment responses, early video access, exclusive content, the social feed — everything.
  • Social Media Connection: I will personally connect with you on whatever social media networks you're active on, so we can stay in contact beyond YouTube.
  • One-on-One Zoom Call: After being a Gold member for at least three months, you'll receive a 30-minute personal video call with me. We'll chat, I'll learn about what drives you and why this content is providing value, and I'll answer any questions you have. Want to deliver a speech and get my honest feedback, expertise, and evaluation? Let's do it. That time is yours.

I cannot express how excited I am to meet and connect with the people this channel is helping. That personal connection is what drives me to create this content in the first place.

The Bottom Line

Whether you're a casual viewer or a future Gold member, you are a valued part of this community. Membership is a wonderful option for those who want it and can afford it, but it is never a requirement. Every view, every comment, every subscription, and every like helps this channel thrive and continue delivering content. Thank you for being here, for watching, and for being part of something that means so much to me. If the membership option works for you, I look forward to connecting on an even deeper level. Have a great day.
